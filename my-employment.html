  • Description:

  • Pebbletree’s brands include Soho66, Quvu, Usomo, Simply66 and Goldfish. These brands provide a cutting edge communication service to their customers.
  • As a technical support advisor my role is to assist customers with any questions or issues that they are having with the services provided by any of Pebbletree’s brands. In this role I am often the first point of contact between the customer and Pebbletree,
  • therefore a high level of customer service is required as all contact between myself and the customer impacts on the image of the brand and the company as a whole. I am tasked with investigating customer's issues and either resolving these issues myself or,
  • if there is an issue I am unable to resolve, escalating those issues to the departments with the required technical expertise to resolve the issue and keep the customer informed of the progress made until a resolution is found.
